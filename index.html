Factory Town District

Factory Town District includes the Factory Town Entertainment Subdistrict Pilot Program, Hialeah, Zoned M-1 (Industrial District). For details, see The City of Hialeah Ordinance No.2020-011. Factory Town currently operates as an entertainment venue hosting music festivals and events multiple times a year. The broader district is in the planning and development phase, with proposed projects including Factory Town Garage, a 638-stall parking facility, and Factory Living, a 235-unit residential building.

The purpose of Factory Town District is to create a mixed-used development that will leverage the Factory Town venue in creating a new neighborhood in a designated Transit-Oriented Development district. The City of Hialeah recently approved a zoning variance change for the Factory Town Garage project to further establish the district.
